About me

Hello! I'm Jinoy, a passionate Data Analysis & Visualization Expert with a keen interest in leveraging Open Data for impactful projects. With a knack for transforming raw data into compelling visual narratives, I specialize in crafting insightful infographics and dynamic visualizations that breathe life into data stories. My dedication extends to contributing to platforms like Wikidata and Wikipedia, enriching datasets for diverse visualization endeavors. Proficient in tools such as OpenRefine for data refinement and platforms like Looker Studio (Google Data Studio), Power BI, Flourish Studio for immersive visualization experiences, I am committed to innovation, volunteering, and exploring new frontiers in technology and mapping projects. Let's collaborate to turn data into actionable insights and meaningful stories.
